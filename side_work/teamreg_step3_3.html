7 Players' Details : Be ready with roles


The Keeper is a crucial position in Quidditch, tasked with guarding the team's goal hoops to prevent the opposing Chasers from scoring. Their exceptional reflexes and agile broomstick maneuvers make them a formidable last line of defense.

The Seeker is a position in Quidditch responsible for locating and catching the elusive Golden Snitch, worth 150 points, which ends the game. They must possess great speed, skill, and determination to secure victory for their team.
